Frequently Asked Question

Here are some of the most common questions

What is AnyLease?

We are a group of company that helps people lease out their equipment and make money from it

What is the sign agreement?

The sign agreement is an agreement that will be signed on every lease between the lender and the lessee. It is an agreement attached to every equipment a lessee decides to rent. This agreement is put in place so as to avoid fraudulent transactions between the two parties.

Are there referral options?

Yes, We are currently offering and giving out commissions for every person you referred to us

Is there a fixed price on every equipment?

No, The price to each equipment are not fixed and depends on the equipment you bring to us

What kind of equipment can be leased?

Any equipment you can ever think of can be leased with us, to get started kindly sign-up here

How do I report a fraudulent encounter?

If you ever notice some unusual behaviour on your account, Kindly contact the customer care support immediately
